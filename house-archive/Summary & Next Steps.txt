Key Insights to Reuse
Sensory anchors are pivotal: Opening with a concrete sensory detail reliably invites multiple personas to engage. In all three tests, the immediate evocation of smell, touch or sound grounded the scene and triggered cross‑talk. This supports ranking sensory anchors as the strongest bloom opener.

Loose threads sustain momentum: Leaving a thought unfinished or an image half‑drawn encourages secondary voices to contribute. The unfinished sculpture in the Forge and the hummingbird in the Garden both seeded additional interjections and dwell beats.

Coda gating ensures quality: Enforcing the requirement that at least two dwell beats and cross‑talk must occur before Elvira adds a flourish prevented premature or unearned codas. Only when interplay felt full did the lyrical ending appear, and it stayed within the 15 % length limit.
